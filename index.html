Welcome to the Jackson Community Food Pantry

The Jackson Community Food Pantry provides food to those in the Jackson Michigan area that have been impacted by financial problems as a result of: unemployment, divorce, disability, health, domestic violence, homelessness, disaster, aging and rising cost of living. We serve and seeks to collaborate with those who share our mission.
